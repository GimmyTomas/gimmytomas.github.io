I consider teaching to be an important part of my involvement in academic life. I always take my teaching duties with extreme enthusiams, but also very seriously. During my PhD I have been a teaching assistant 7 times.

General Relativity

I have been a teaching assistant for the Master's course taught by professor Daniel Baumann in 2021, 2022 and 2023. This included leading tutorials and writing many of the exercises and the exam problems, which can be found appended to the lecture notes.

GRAPPA Student Seminars

I have been a teaching assistant for the Master's course taught by professor Gianfranco Bertone in 2021, 2022, 2023 and 2024. I have lectured on Dark Matter candidates and searches, including: WIMPs, PBHs, axions, ULBs, MOND, indirect detection, direct detection and GW searches.
